JOURNAL

Our Story, One Chapter at a Time

2023

The Beginning

It all started with a simple craving and a recipe scribbled on a napkin. We were just two people in a tiny kitchen, figuring out how to make the perfect cookie. The first batch was far from perfect — but it tasted like the start of something beautiful.

2024

Overnight Oats

When our mornings got busier with the little ones, we discovered the magic of overnight oats — a breakfast you prepare with love the night before. The kids loved picking their own toppings. It became our morning ritual, and soon our friends were asking for jars of their own.

Christmas 2025

Christmas '25

Our most magical season yet. We baked over 200 cookie boxes for friends, family, and new customers who felt like family. The house smelled like cinnamon and chocolate for weeks. Watching the kids tape ribbons onto each box reminded us why we started — to share joy, one bite at a time.
