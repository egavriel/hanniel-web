The Beginning

It all started with a simple craving and a recipe scribbled on a napkin. We were just two people in a tiny kitchen, figuring out how to make the perfect cookie. The first batch was far from perfect — but it tasted like the start of something beautiful.

2024

Overnight Oats

When our mornings got busier with the little ones, we discovered the magic of overnight oats — a breakfast you prepare with love the night before. The kids loved picking their own toppings. It became our morning ritual, and soon friends were asking for jars of their own.
